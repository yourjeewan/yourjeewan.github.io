Plant breeding is my passion

My involvement at TAMU Potato Breeding can be accesssed via Potato Breeding.

I am focusing on implementing molecular markers and tools to improve the breeding efficiency of potatoes. There is an urgent need to speed up the genetic improvement in potatoes because conventional cultivar development often takes a long period (more than 10 years).
